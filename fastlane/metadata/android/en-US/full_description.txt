RoadTripRadar overlays live weather radar imagery on an interactive map, helping you see precipitation along your route at a glance.

Features:
- Navigate using bearing to your destination instead of rigid turn-by-turn instructions. Perfect for explorers and people that like to wander!
- Live animated weather radar from RainViewer with adjustable opacity and playback controls. Perfect for motorcyclists that want notice to reroute around a storm or gear up.
- Compass button toggles between north-up and your previous heading.
- Multiple map styles: Liberty (light), Dark, and Color Dark.
- Speed readout with metric and imperial units, adjustable font size.
- Point of interest search by name or category (29 categories including gas stations, EV charging, restaurants, campsites, and more).
- Drop a custom POI by long-pressing anywhere on the map.
- Navigation widget showing direction arrow, distance, and name of your selected POI.
- Distance rings centered on your location that adapt to zoom level.
- Configurable UI sizing for speed and navigation displays.
- Keep-screen-on mode for hands-free use while driving.
- Open source, no tracking, no accounts, no ads.
