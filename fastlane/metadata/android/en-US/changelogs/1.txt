Initial release of RoadTripRadar.

- Live animated weather radar overlay from RainViewer
- Bearing-based navigation to points of interest
- POI search by name or category
- Drop POI by long-pressing the map
- Distance rings around your location
- Speed readout in metric or imperial
- Three map styles: Liberty, Dark, Color Dark
- Compass button with heading toggle
- Adjustable radar opacity
- Configurable UI sizing
- Keep-screen-on mode
