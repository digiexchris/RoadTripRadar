RoadTripRadar overlays live weather radar imagery on an interactive map, helping you see precipitation along your route at a glance.

Features:
- Navigate using bearing to your destination instead of rigid turn by turn instructions. Perfect for explorers and people that like to wander!
- Live animated weather radar from RainViewer. Perfect for motorcyclists that want to have notice to either reroute to dodge a storm, or gear up.
- Adjustable radar opacity
- Multiple map styles (Liberty, Dark, Color Dark)
- Speed readout with metric and imperial units
- Point of interest search by name or category
- Custom POI by touch and holding on a position on the map
- Distance rings centered on your location
- Configurable UI sizing
- Open source, no tracking, no accounts
