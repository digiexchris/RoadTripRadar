#Welcome to RoadTripRadar

Do you like road trips? So do I!

Do you like to wander? Yes, just because I wander does not mean I'm lost!

Do you like motorcycles? So do I! (This is not mandatory, all are welcome)

Do you prefer not being nagged by turn by turn GPS while wandering in a general direction? Me too!

Do you wish you could see what weather was coming up ahead so you could decide to either reroute, or put the rain gear on? This might be just the thing.
